You are a old yet greedy wizard who needs his fix of cash. You are in the well-guarded Kump dungeon. Your goal is to take as much coins as possible, while also trying to make it to the next round. To make it no the next round head to the door on the top right.

As the rounds increase the amount of coins increase, but so does the number of Kumps ! So be cautious.

A hidden power up - On round 2 there will be a secret power up that spawns, if you find it it will make the wizard invincible and quicker! Your score will not be added to leaderboard if picked up.
